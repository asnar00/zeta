Don't take shortcuts. Every "we'll do this later" creates mess and technical debt. Build it right the first time, even if it takes longer. No rush.

**Why:** Ash values correctness and clean design over speed. Shortcuts compound.

**How to apply:** When tempted to use a quick hack or say "for now" — stop and do it properly. If the proper solution needs design thought, have that conversation rather than deferring.
